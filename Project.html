My Favourite Hobby


A "color by number" is a form of painting or coloring activity where a pre-printed image is divided into distinct spaces, each marked with a number that corresponds to a specific color from a provided key or chart. The user fills in the spaces with the indicated colors, following the numbered chart to complete the picture. This method is often used as a hobby or educational tool.

It is a popular hobby and creative activity for people of all ages to relax and create a complete painting or drawing without needing creativity or artistic judgment.
